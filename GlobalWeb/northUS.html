Nothern US

Northwetern: Idaho, Montana, Oregon, Washington, and Wyoming

  • Cool winters, mountains heavy snowfall, wet and dry near the sea
  • Sunny summer, not too hot
  • Convenient for tourism and resort activities

High Plains: Kansas, Minnesota, Nebraska, North Dakota, and South Dakota

  • Cold and freezing winters
  • Sunny summer and hot

Midwest: Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kentucky, Michigan, Missouri, Ohio, and Wisconsin

  • Cold and freezing winters
  • Sunny summer and hot.

Northeast: New England/Mid Atlantic

  • Rain, ice, snow, and storm in the winters
  • Humidity summer, lightning
  • Cooler and relatively more pleasant in spring and autumn
