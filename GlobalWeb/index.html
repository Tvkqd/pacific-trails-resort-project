Travel to a Tropical Country

Climate is considered one of the important factors in the decision-making process to travel in other country.
With a large area and diverse geographical features, the US has a variety of different climates that characterize different regions across the country.
Compare to the US, the weather in Vietnam is more simple with mainly two seasons in a year: wet and dry.
