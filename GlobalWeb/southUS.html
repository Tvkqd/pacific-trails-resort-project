Southern US

Southeast: Tennessee, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Alabama and Florida

  • Cool winters and cool night temperatures
  • Hot, humid and rainy summer
  • Thunderstorms and hurricanes in the spring

Southwestern: California, Nevada, Utah, Colorado, Arizona, and New Mexico

  • Hot and warm all year round
  • Influenced by the cool air of the Pacific Ocean
  • Difficult to recognize the change of seasons
